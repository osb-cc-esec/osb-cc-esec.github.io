OSB IoT Device Manager User Manual

Classically embedded systems have long been considered particularly secure because they had no connection to the Internet and they did not yet produce any data that would enable new types of business models today. Popular applications are household appliances, cars or agricultural machinery in which computers are installed. The further development of the Internet of Things (IoT) and the resulting new connectivity to the Internet and data clouds have resulted in numerous advantages. However, there was also the risk that internet-enabled devices could become vulnerable to cyber attacks.

With the IoT Device Manager from The Embedded Security Experts, we offer you an IoT security solution to manage IoT devices and secure them in the best possible way against data manipulation, device decommissioning, product piracy, data theft and the misuse of IoT systems by building an unwanted one Botnets. Since no two setups are the same, we develop individually tailored IoT security solutions for you that are tailored to your needs.
